To understand the meanings of different terms in our codebase, we maintain this glossary.

## User

A user is a representation of a human entity. Users have authentication by login.

## Organization

An organization is an entity that defines collective ownership.

Users can belong to one or more organizations.

## Team

A team is a group within an organization that defines shared permissions over resources owned by the governing organization.

## Project

A project is a publishing identity. This can be an artist, a label, or a publisher. Each user gets one auto-generated project on sign-up.

Projects are owned by users or organizations.

## Resource

A resource is a creative work. This can be a song, an artwork, a press release, or even an album of other resources.

Resources are attributed to one or more projects.

Resources are owned by users or organizations.
